About Me

I am a Ph.D. Scholar from Department of Computer Science, University of Delhi. Currently, I am working under Prof. Vasudha Bhatnagar in the field of Text Analytics. I am a member of the Network Science Lab lead by Prof. Bhatnagar. My research interests include Text Mining, Graph Analytics, Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, and Discourse Analysis.

The goal of my PhD work is to develop computational methods for automatic analysis of long documents for the purpose of quantitative and qualitative assessment. Recently I worked on the problem of Automatic Keyword Extraction, and proposed a novel, parameterless, graph-based keyword extraction algorithm and its language-agnostic variant. I have also worked on semi-automatic title construction and supervised keyword extraction using graph-theoretic methods. Currently, I am working on the problem of coherence analysis. I intend to apply complex network based techniques to quantify coherence of text documents.

Apart from research, I enjoy reading fictions and classic novels, watching food and travel vlogs, and writing poems and short stories.

Projects

Coherence Analysis

I am currently working on a project on coherence analysis. My objective is to analyse scientific articles on the basis of their coherence, and quantify the measure of coherence. I intend to explore complex network based properties to devise algorithms for computing the coherence score.

Supervised Keyword Extraction

We used classical ML algorithms to engineer solutions for automatically extracting keywords from single documents. We transformed the text to a complex network representation and extracted node properties as features. The proposed method works on all documents, irrespective of the domain, collection, or language of the document.

View Project

Semi-automatic Title Construction

We developed a semi-automatic system for identifying and recommending keywords for inclusion in the title of a scientific manuscript. Here, the keyword extraction phase is automatic and title construction from extracted keyword is manual. For extracting keywords, we induced supervised models using a graph-theoretic feature set.

View Project

Unsupervised Keyword Extraction

We have engineered solutions for unsupervised, graph-based keyword extraction from single documents. We proposed a novel, parameterless, graph-based keyword extraction algorithm (sCAKE) and its language-agnostic variant (LAKE). We also proposed a context-aware graph construction method and a semantic connectivity based word scoring method.

View Project

Revenue Management System

As a major project during my graduation (MCA), I have developed "Revenue Management System for APDCL" for Assam Power Distribution Company Limited (APDCL), Assam, India. It was a six (6) month project work and was developed using JSP as front-end and MySQL as back-end. The objective of this project was to design an efficient database to store information regarding revenue collection of APDCL and to build a web-based application to effectively view, manipulate, and aggregate the stored information.

iRAT-a review aggregation tool

As a minor project during my graduation (MCA), I have developed "iRAT - a review aggregation tool" which was an NLP and sentiment Analysis based project for movie classification. The objective of this project was to analyse movie reviews and assign an aggregated sentiment polarity (positive, negative, and neutral) to the reviews. It was a six (6) month project and was developed using JSP. I have used bag-of-words model for document representation and polarity dataset v1.0 for evaluation.
